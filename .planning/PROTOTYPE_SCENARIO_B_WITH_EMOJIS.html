🔐 How it works

Brute Force tests every possible combination of characters sequentially (aaa, aab, aac...). Extremely slow for long passwords but doesn't depend on any prior knowledge.

O(charset^length)

Why it's slow: The number of combinations grows exponentially with password length. A 10-character password with 94 possible characters = 94^10 combinations.

Effective against: Short passwords (< 8 chars) or simple character sets.
